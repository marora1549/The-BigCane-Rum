Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

Where can I purchase The BigCane products?

The BigCane products are available at premium liquor stores across the Caribbean, North America, and select international markets. You can also purchase our products through authorized online retailers. Use our store locator to find the nearest retailer.

Do you ship internationally?

Due to varying alcohol regulations across countries, we don't ship directly from our website. However, many of our authorized retailers offer international shipping where legally permitted. We recommend checking with your local premium spirits retailers.

How can I collaborate with The BigCane for events or promotions?

We love collaborating with like-minded brands and influencers who share our passion for Caribbean culture and premium experiences. Please email us at partnerships@thebigcane.com with your proposal, and our partnerships team will review your request.

Are The BigCane products vegan-friendly?

Yes, all The BigCane rum products are vegan-friendly. Our production process does not involve any animal products or by-products. We're committed to maintaining high standards that respect various dietary preferences.

What's the best way to store The BigCane rum?

To preserve the quality and flavor profile of our rum, we recommend storing bottles in a cool, dark place away from direct sunlight and heat sources. Unlike wine, spirits don't continue to age in the bottle, so there's no need to store them horizontally. Once opened, our rums can maintain their quality for years if properly sealed and stored.
