Caribbean Sunset

About This Cocktail

The Caribbean Sunset is our signature cocktail, inspired by the breathtaking sunset views across the Caribbean islands. This refreshing drink combines the smooth notes of our Silver Rum with tropical mango and zesty lime, finished with a spectacular sunset gradient created by the grenadine.

Strength
Sweetness
Tanginess
Crowd Pleaser

Ingredients

  • 2 oz The Big Cane Silver Rum
  • 1 oz fresh mango puree
  • 1/2 oz fresh lime juice
  • 1/2 oz grenadine
  • Ice cubes
  • Mango slice and lime wheel for garnish
  • Optional: 1 tsp sugar (adjust to taste)

Instructions

  1. Fill a cocktail shaker halfway with ice cubes.
  2. Add The Big Cane Silver Rum, mango puree, lime juice, and optional sugar.
  3. Shake vigorously for about 15 seconds until well-chilled.
  4. Strain into a chilled glass filled with fresh ice.
  5. Slowly pour grenadine down the inside of the glass to create a layered sunset effect.
  6. Garnish with a mango slice and lime wheel on the rim.

Bartender Notes

For a non-alcoholic version, substitute the rum with coconut water and a splash of vanilla extract. The grenadine should be added last for the signature sunset gradient effect. For the best visual appeal, pour the grenadine slowly over the back of a spoon to create beautiful layers.
