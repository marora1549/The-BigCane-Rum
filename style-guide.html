Brand Overview

The BigCane brand embodies premium Caribbean spirits with a vibrant nightclub aesthetic. The brand values luxury, celebration, rhythm, and craftsmanship. It captures the essence of island luxury with a modern twist.

Brand Voice

  • Confident: The brand speaks with authority and expertise.
  • Vibrant: Language is lively, energetic, and conveys the party vibe.
  • Premium: A touch of sophistication without being pretentious.
  • Authentic: True to Caribbean roots while embracing global nightlife.

Logo Usage

The logo should always have adequate spacing around it (minimum clearance equal to the height of the letter 'B' in the logo). Never distort, recolor, or alter the logo without approval.

Usage Guidelines

  • Maintain proper hierarchy with headers (h1 through h4).
  • Use body text for main content and small text for supplementary information.
  • Keep lines of text to a maximum width of 70 characters for optimal readability.
  • Use accent text sparingly for emphasis and highlighting important quotes.
  • Ensure sufficient contrast between text and background colors.

Buttons

Buttons are a key interactive element that guide users through the site. They feature vibrant colors, subtle animations, and clear text to drive action.

Primary Button

Primary buttons use a gradient background with our neon pink color. They include a subtle glow animation on hover to enhance interactivity.

<button class="button glow-pulse">Discover More</button>

Secondary Button

Secondary buttons use a transparent background with a border. They're used for less prominent actions.

<button class="button button--secondary">Learn More</button>

Text Button

Text buttons are used for navigation links and minor actions. They often include an icon for visual emphasis.

<button class="button button--text">Read More <i class="fas fa-arrow-right"></i></button>

Button Sizes

Buttons come in three sizes to accommodate different use cases and screen sizes.

<button class="button button--small">Small</button> <button class="button">Default</button> <button class="button button--large">Large</button>

Animations

Animations add life and energy to the website, reinforcing the vibrant nightclub aesthetic of the brand. Use animations purposefully to enhance the user experience.

Hover Effects

Hover effects provide immediate feedback to users and enhance interactivity. Use them consistently on buttons and interactive elements.

Scroll Animations

Elements can animate into view as users scroll down the page. Common animations include:

  • reveal-from-bottom - Element fades in while moving up
  • reveal-from-left - Element fades in while moving right
  • reveal-from-right - Element fades in while moving left
  • fade-in - Element simply fades in without movement

Scroll animations should be subtle and enhance the content rather than distract from it.

Accessibility

Making our website accessible to all users is a priority. Follow these guidelines to ensure the site is usable by people with various disabilities.

Text and Color

  • Maintain a minimum contrast ratio of 4.5:1 for normal text and 3:1 for large text.
  • Don't rely solely on color to convey information.
  • Use proper heading hierarchy (h1, h2, h3, etc.).

Images and Media

  • Provide alternative text for all images using the alt attribute.
  • Use descriptive text that conveys the image's purpose and content.
  • For decorative images, use empty alt attributes (alt="").

Forms and Controls

  • Label all form fields with descriptive text.
  • Ensure all interactive elements are keyboard accessible.
  • Provide clear error messages for form validation.

ARIA Attributes

  • Use ARIA landmarks to identify regions of the page.
  • Add aria-label or aria-labelledby to elements without visible text.
  • Use aria-expanded, aria-hidden, and other ARIA attributes appropriately.
