The Man Behind The Cane

Sunil Gehani, a visionary entrepreneur with roots deeply embedded in Caribbean soil, founded The BigCane with a singular mission: to share the vibrant spirit of the islands with the world. His journey began with a passion for craftsmanship and a profound respect for traditional distillation methods, leading him to explore the rich heritage of Caribbean rum-making. Today, that passion has evolved into The BigCane – a brand that embodies the perfect fusion of island authenticity, electrifying nightlife, and modern luxury.
