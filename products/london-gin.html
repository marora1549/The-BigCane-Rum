London Dry Gin

Caribbean Botanicals, London Tradition

Our London Dry Gin brings a bold Caribbean twist to a classic spirit. Infused with traditional juniper and coriander, but elevated with exotic botanicals sourced directly from the Caribbean islands. Experience the lush notes of hibiscus, allspice, and citrus creating a vibrant, aromatic profile that's perfect for crafting extraordinary cocktails or savoring in a sophisticated G&T.

Cocktail Recipes
Appearance
Crystal clear with a slight silvery sheen. Perfect clarity that catches and reflects light beautifully.
Nose
Bold juniper forward with bright citrus notes. Exotic hibiscus and allspice provide unique floral and spicy undertones unlike any other gin.
Palate
Complex and well-balanced. Traditional dry gin notes accompanied by tropical undertones. Piney juniper leads, followed by island citrus, subtle coconut, and aromatic spice.
Finish
Long, crisp finish with lingering spice notes. The tropical botanicals provide a unique warm sensation that's both refreshing and complex.

Botanical Selection

We hand-select 14 botanicals, combining traditional London Dry ingredients with exotic Caribbean varieties. Our master distiller personally sources rare hibiscus flowers, native Caribbean allspice berries, island-grown citrus peels, and aromatic herbs to create a unique botanical blend.

Vapor Infusion

Unlike many gins that steep botanicals directly in the spirit, we use our proprietary vapor infusion method. Botanicals are suspended in copper baskets above our custom-designed still, allowing the rising vapors to extract the purest, most delicate flavors and aromas without any harshness.

Small Batch Distillation

We distill in small batches of no more than 500 liters at a time in our copper pot still. This artisanal approach allows our master distiller to carefully control the entire process, making precise cuts to capture only the "heart" of the distillation where the finest flavors reside.

Pure Island Water

We reduce our gin to bottling strength using only the purest filtered rainwater collected on the island. This water, naturally filtered through volcanic rock layers, adds a distinctive mineral character that enhances the spirit's mouthfeel and flavor integration.

Resting Period

Before bottling, our gin rests for three weeks in neutral vessels. This crucial step allows the botanicals to fully harmonize and the flavors to marry perfectly, creating a remarkably smooth and balanced spirit that maintains both the classic gin character and our distinctive Caribbean twist.
