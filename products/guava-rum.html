Guava Rum

Tropical Sweetness, Caribbean Spirit

Immerse yourself in the luscious taste of the Caribbean with our Guava Rum. We've infused our premium rum with handpicked tropical guavas, creating a perfectly balanced spirit with sweet, tangy notes and a velvety smooth finish. Perfect for crafting exotic cocktails or enjoying over ice on a warm evening, this limited edition rum captures the essence of island paradise.

Cocktail Recipes
Appearance
Light pink-amber hue with a subtle rosy glow. Crystal clear with a medium viscosity that creates beautiful legs on the glass.
Nose
Vibrant tropical guava dominates, with supporting notes of vanilla, light caramel, and hints of ripe pineapple. A subtle floral character adds complexity and elegance.
Palate
Rich guava flavor with perfect sweetness balance. The natural tropical fruit notes are complemented by vanilla cream, light oak influences, and a hint of warm spice that prevents it from being cloying.
Finish
Medium-long finish with lingering guava sweetness that gradually gives way to subtle rum warmth. Pleasant tropical fruit notes persist without being overpowering or artificial.

Fruit Selection

We source only the freshest, ripest pink guavas from local Caribbean farms. Each fruit is hand-selected at peak ripeness, ensuring the perfect balance of sweetness and tanginess. Our guavas are harvested in small batches throughout the season to maintain the highest quality.

Premium Rum Base

Our signature silver rum serves as the foundation, aged for a minimum of two years in oak barrels. This creates a smooth, balanced base with subtle vanilla and caramel notes that perfectly complement the tropical guava flavors without overpowering them.

Natural Infusion

Rather than using artificial flavors, we employ a proprietary cold-infusion process where the fresh guava puree is immersed in our rum for several weeks. This gentle method extracts the vibrant flavor compounds and natural sugars from the fruit while preserving their delicate aromatic qualities.

Harmonization Period

After the initial infusion, we allow the rum to rest for an additional 30 days. During this critical harmonization phase, the flavors meld together, creating a cohesive flavor profile where the guava and rum notes integrate perfectly rather than competing with each other.

Filtration & Bottling

The final product undergoes a special cold-filtration process to remove any sediment while preserving the full flavor profile. Each bottle is then filled, sealed, and individually numbered by hand, reflecting the limited production of this specialty rum and our commitment to artisanal craftsmanship.
