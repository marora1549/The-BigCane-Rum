Gift FAQs

Common questions about our gift sets and gifting options

Do you offer gift wrapping services?

Yes, all of our gift sets come in premium packaging designed for gifting. We also offer additional gift wrapping options at checkout, including custom ribbons, gift tags, and personalized messages.

Can I ship gift sets internationally?

Due to varying alcohol regulations and shipping restrictions, international shipping availability depends on the destination country. Please contact our customer service team for specific information about shipping to your desired location.

How far in advance should I order for a specific occasion?

We recommend placing standard gift set orders at least 7-10 business days before your desired delivery date. For custom gift sets or corporate bulk orders, please allow 14-21 business days to ensure timely delivery and proper customization.

Do you offer corporate bulk discounts?

Yes, we offer special pricing for corporate bulk orders. Discounts vary based on order quantity and customization requirements. Please contact our corporate gifting team at corporate@thebigcane.com for a personalized quote.

Can I include a personalized message with my gift?

Absolutely! All gift orders include the option to add a complimentary personalized message card. Simply enter your message at checkout, and we'll include it with your gift. For more extensive personalization options, including custom labels or engraving, please select a custom gift option.
